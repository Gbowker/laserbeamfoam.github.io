# laserMeltFoam

![alt text](lmf_simulation.gif)


`laserMeltFoam` is a solver for simulating the laser melting process in powder bed fusion (PBF) additive manufacturing. It is part of the OpenFOAM framework and is designed to model the thermal and fluid dynamics involved in the laser melting of metal powders.
